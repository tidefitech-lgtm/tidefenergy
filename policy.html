02

Site Inventory

Assets, equipment and deployment readiness

TIDEF Energy maintains a comprehensive inventory of subsea tooling, ROV systems, and support equipment. This documentation supports operational planning, mobilisation and asset integrity. Availability of specific equipment is subject to client scheduling, inspection and service readiness.

03

NCDMB Framework

Local content and regulatory compliance

TIDEF Energy aligns its operations with the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board (NCDMB) framework. This includes local labour sourcing, capacity building, reporting and partner development practices designed to support national industry growth and compliance with Nigerian offshore energy regulations.

04

ISO 9001 Standard

Quality management and continual improvement

Our systems follow ISO 9001 principles for quality management, process control, and performance measurement. We focus on client satisfaction, risk-based decision making, and continual improvement throughout project planning, execution and aftercare support.

05

Privacy Compliance

Data protection and communications

TIDEF Energy is committed to protecting visitor and customer data. Personal information collected through enquiries, emails, or site interactions is handled with care and used only for legitimate business purposes. We do not sell or share personal data without consent.
